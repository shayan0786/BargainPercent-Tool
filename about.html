About Discount Calculator

A professional tool designed to help small businesses calculate and display discounts with transparency and ease

Why This Tool Exists

During my interactions with local shopkeepers, I noticed three common problems:

  • Shopkeepers struggle to quickly calculate discount percentages during sales
  • Customers often don't understand how much they're actually saving
  • Rounded pricing (like ₹665 → ₹600) makes percentage calculations confusing

This tool solves all these problems with a simple, intuitive interface that shows customers exactly what discount they're getting.

Key Features

Instant Calculations

Get accurate discount percentages in real-time as you type, eliminating manual calculation errors.

Customer Display

Professional display shows customers exactly what they saved in clear, simple terms.

Multiple Items

Calculate discounts for entire purchases with multiple items at different prices.
